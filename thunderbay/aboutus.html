About Us

Born out of a passion for training and academies, this hobby became a reality in the lives of the co-founders. After years of training and actively participating in a training routine, we realize the lack of knowledge that many people who attend the gym have. Then came the idea of creating an academy with full customer support, who cares and seeks innovative methods to achieve the results of their students, so the thunder bay gym was born.

A word from us

"We believe that a good training environment should always be accompanied by a good professional. We don't see it as a luxury, but a necessity. With that in mind, we will do everything we can to make sure you follow your plan day after day."

"Good health starts with good training, and good training is done by specialized professionals. At thunder bay gym we believe in "divide to conquer", where you can share your dream with us, that together we will achieve success."
