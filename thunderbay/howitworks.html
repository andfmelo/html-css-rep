How it works

1. You choose the plan that best fits your routine / reality. We have three subscription options and they will all help you achieve your goals. Part of the work you leave with us without having to worry about how to do your training, we will do it for you.

2. We will contact you to create a profile, taking your main data: weight, height, age and other data in order to create a monthly plan for you. That way we can develop training plans, diet tips and other useful stuff to keep you feed up.

3. In the following months, access to professionals is online, depending on the plan. That way you can choose the plan based on your training frequency and availability. It is important to note that the use of the app is essential at this time.

4. schedule monthly analyzes with a professional directly in the app. At that moment you can discuss your plan, the results and what you expect from your training, to align expectations. Again, look at your plans and see which one fits you best.

5. enjoy your workout!
